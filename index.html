jQueryMobile - AlertBox

An "alert" box automator for jQueryMobile

AlertBox is a timed JavaScript "alert();" replacement, with automation, for jQM

jQM-AlertBox

This was written for a few reasons:

  1. I found a *really* old question on stackoverflow and thought it was new
  2. I wanted to see if I could crank out a plugin in under 2 hours. I could

Anyway, this just automates that little window that either disappeared after approx 5 seconds when you opened this page, or that you clicked close on faster than that.
